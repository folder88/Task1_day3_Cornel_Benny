The Best Pavlova

  • In a large bowl,beat egg whites on hight speed until soft peaks form.Add 3/4 cup of the sugar gradually, while continuing to whip. Make sure sugar is completely dissolved. Mix together the remaining 1/4 cup sugar with the cornstarch; lightly fold into meringue with lemon juice.
  • Spread a layer of meringue to fit circle on parchment, approximately 1/4 inch thick. With the remainder of the mixture, pipe or spoon swirls around the edges to form a shallow bowl shape.
